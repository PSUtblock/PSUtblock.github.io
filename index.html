Welcome!

I am Travis Block, a computer science student at Portland State University. This is my portfolio site that shares information about me and some of the projects I am currently working on. I do not have much content but I hope that in the next few years I will have many more examples to share.


Future Goals as of November 2021:


Expanding on My Educational Content

A lot of my web development journey comes from my desires to tap more into my ability of creating educational content. In my past I have been an Instructional Designer and I have made quite a few trainings using Adobe Captivate. Captivate is some pretty cool software and is built off javascript, html, and other web focused tools. Additionaly, the softwares interface is akin to that of PowerPoint, but with it's simplicities came barriers that did not really allow me to be efficient and speedy with my development. So I started looking more at the core of the product to see what I could really do ungoverned by software limitations. To say it has been a lot to take in is putting it lightly, but I am confident that I will be able to accomplish what I would like to do in more effective ways once I get the hang of everything.


Pushing my Animation

I am very new to web development, but I think it is an amazing way to convey a message and share your style. I have been into animation for a long time, but never really challenged myself to create my own. So, what better platform to start? Thanks to my class project, I have developed this site, and now I can use it as a playground to try new things. In particular Animation! Aside from that, I am an educator, so I am hoping to use everything I have learned to develop interesting learning content that is accessible through the power of the world wide web!
