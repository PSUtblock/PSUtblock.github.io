About Me:

Hello, My name is Travis Block and I am currently a student studying at Portland State Univeristy. I have a wide variety of interests and hobbies, and often struggle to find which to fully commit to. I have a passion for education and whatever I can do to connect students with the content they are learning is pivotal for me. So, as I think towards the future of education, the use of technology grows more and more. Because of this, I study computer science to bolster my love of education and merge the two disciplines together to make many learning tools and interactions from students. From flashcards to small educational games, or to just a well designed site, I would like to create them all to ensure someone can learn from my creations.

I have only recently began this journey and I hope that in the coming months or years, I will have something to show for it. At this moment I have only a few examples of what I have been working on, but admitidly more archiving and storing my projects on github is needed, but thankfully I am getting the hang of that!

My Career Dreams:

My dream goal is to be in a position that allows me to work with other instructors and develop curriculums with digital integration and curate thoughtful content for students to understand concepts. I am a firm believer that the environment plays a role in someones attainment of knowledge, and see the digital realm as a space that can be neutral and offer many learning styles at the convienience to those wanting to learn. While I do not have This position now, I do enjoy creating things in my free time that would reflect what I want to do.
